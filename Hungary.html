Budapest, Hungary There are a number of incredible sites to see in the capitol city of Hungary. Budapest (pronounced Budapesht) is known as the "Paris of the East", and it holds rich history and beautiful memories. Here are a few recommendations for you as you experience the joys of the Hungarian culture!

  1. Places to Visit
    • Halászbásztya, or "Fisherman's Bastien
    • St. Stephen's Bazsilika
    • Hero's Square
    • The Parlaiment Building
    • Dinner Cruise on the Danube
    • The Soviet Union Statue Graveyard
    • The Opera House
  2. Food to Try
    • Rose Fagyi by St.Stephen's Bazsilika
    • Hortobagyi Palacsinta
    • Paprikás Csirke
  3. Best Transportation
    • Use the Metro! Daily tickets are cheap and get you everywhere
    • The metro tickets work for buses around the city
    • Villámos - like a metro above the ground, these yellow trolleys give you the best view of the city
      (Take the one across Erzébeth Bridge)
